Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what **Joystick Forest**
collects, why, and how it is handled.
By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Data
We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, or phone number).

### Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, third-party services (primarily Google AdMob) may automatically collect certain anonymous information, including:
- Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- IP address
- App usage statistics (for ad serving and frequency capping)

### Local Storage
The App stores your preferences and settings (such as favorite games or custom lists) **locally on your device** only. This data is not sent to us or any third party.

### WebView and Third-Party Games
When you launch a game, the App opens it in a WebView (or external browser). In this case, the game’s website or platform (e.g., itch.io, CrazyGames, etc.) may collect its own data according to their privacy policies. We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party sites.

## Use of Information

We and our third-party partners (Google AdMob) use the collected information primarily for:
- Serving personalized and non-personalized advertisements
- Measuring ad performance
- Improving the stability and performance of the App
- Complying with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.  
We may share anonymous technical data only with:
- Google (for AdMob advertising services)
- Legal authorities, if required by law

## Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

The App is intended for a general audience and contains games that may include mature (18+) content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we may have collected information from a child, please contact us.

## Your Rights

Since we collect almost no personal data, your main options are:
- Reset your advertising ID in Android settings (this limits personalized ads)
- Clear the App’s local data / cache through device settings
- Uninstall the App

We take reasonable measures to protect any information processed by the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
