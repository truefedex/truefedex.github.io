Privacy Policy for ANMA – Alternative Navigation Methods for Android TV


Last updated: 30.04.2026


ANMA (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights when using the App.


1. Overview


ANMA is designed to provide alternative navigation methods for Android TV devices, including virtual cursor control and head gesture interaction.


The App is built with a strong focus on privacy. It does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.


2. Information Collection

2.1 Personal Data


The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as:


name

email address

location

contacts

2.2 Camera Usage


ANMA may request access to the device camera for the purpose of detecting head movements and enabling gesture-based navigation.


Camera data is processed locally on the device only

No images, videos, or biometric data are stored or transmitted

No camera data leaves your device

2.3 Accessibility Services


ANMA uses Android Accessibility Services to:


simulate navigation input

control focus and UI interaction

provide alternative navigation methods


Accessibility data:


is processed locally on the device

is not collected, stored, or transmitted


The App does not monitor, collect, or share sensitive user data through Accessibility APIs.


2.4 Analytics and Tracking


The App does not use:


analytics services

tracking tools

advertising SDKs

3. Data Sharing


The App does not share any data with third parties.


4. Data Storage


The App does not store personal data. Any temporary processing (e.g., camera frames) happens in memory and is not persisted.


5. Security


Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, risks related to data breaches are minimized.


6. Children’s Privacy


The App does not knowingly collect data from children.


7. Your Rights


Since the App does not collect personal data, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete.


8. Changes to This Policy


This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date.
